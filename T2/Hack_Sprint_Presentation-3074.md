# Project 3074: Hack Sprint Presentation
----

## Challenge

This is the presentation half of your Hack Sprint! After the development period for the Hack Sprint, you will show what you have accomplished and the challenges you’ve faced while completing this project.

You will have 5 minutes to present your tool or game with your team.


----
## Tasks
---
### 0. Presentation Content

Post a link to your presentation slides.

Slide may include the following:

- Project name and team members
- Purpose/function
- Inspiration
- Design process
- Challenges you ran into / overcame
- Demonstration (pre-recorded and in your slide deck)


---
### 1. Presentation Delivery

Present your presentation live! Every team member must speak.
